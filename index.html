What is Craniosacral Therapy?

Craniosacral therapy (CST) is a gentle, hands-on technique that uses light touch to release tension around the body's connective tissues, particularly focusing on the head, neck, and spine, to promote pain relief and overall health.

— Cleveland Clinic. "Craniosacral Therapy."
